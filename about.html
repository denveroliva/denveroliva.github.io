About ESGA & COSGA

ESGA & COSGA Colegio De San Gabriel of Caloocan Incorporation is a school that offers an excellent approach to education for children, with regulations in place to maintain peace and order in the school. The teachers have a strong connection with the students, fostering a supportive environment to prevent conflicts and misunderstandings among the learners.

In our school, every classroom is air-conditioned and cleaned daily by our staff, ensuring that the learning environment is safe and free from harmful bacteria, which helps prevent illnesses. COSGA also supports the Learner's Voucher program, allowing students to save on tuition fees, which is especially beneficial for middle-class families who want their children to attend a quality school.

Additionally, ESGA & COSGA supports various sports like badminton, basketball, volleyball, and more, providing students the opportunity to showcase their talents and skills in these activities.

COSGA also offers technical-vocational programs, Alternative Learning System (ALS), and TESDA certifications, which are designed to help students gain hands-on experience and skills, making it easier for them to apply for jobs and build successful careers.
